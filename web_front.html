About Me

Hi, my name is Mahathi Mandelli, and I'm an international graduate student from India. I'm currently enjoying my first-time experience in a new country. I hail from the southern part of India, which is known for its diverse cultures. When I was younger, I had a passion for three things: art, music, and cooking. I have a great love for music and enjoy singing and listening to all kinds of genres. One of my hobbies is creating playlists on Spotify. I have been learning Carnatic music for the past 11 years and have performed in a few live concerts with my team back in India. I also enjoy drawing pencil and pen sketches, and experimenting with food is my go-to stress reliever. I believe that good food equals good mood. Apart from this, I love traveling and exploring new cafes. My happy time is when I'm wandering around new streets with my earphones plugged in, listening to my favorite playlists. That's a little about me!
